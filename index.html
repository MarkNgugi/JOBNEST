HOW WE WORK

After successfully applying and gaining acceptance, you will be provided with unique M-codes that you can use to refer individuals to our website. These unique M-codes will not only assist us in identifying you but also ensure that you receive your rightful commission without any complications.

 

Assignments Commision

In this option, if you refer someone to our website and they make a payment for an assignment service, you will receive a 20% commission on the amount they pay. For example, if someone pays Ksh.200 for an assignment service, you will earn a commission of Ksh.40. Additionally, if you successfully refer five or more people within a five-day period, your commission rate will be increased to 35% for the following two days.

Info From : MacHub

 

Projects Commision

In this option, if you refer someone to our website and they purchase a project service, you will receive a 20% commission based on their payment. For example, if a person pays Ksh. 250 for a project service, you will earn a commission of Ksh. 50. Additionally, if you refer four or more people within a span of five days, your commission rate will increase to 25% for the subsequent two days.

Info From : MacHub

 

OS Installation Commision

In this option, if you refer someone to our website and they purchase our OS installation service, you will receive a 20% commission based on their payment. For instance, if someone pays Ksh.200 for our OS installation service, you will earn a commission of Ksh.40. Additionally, if you refer three or more people within five days, your commission rate will increase to 35% for the next two days.

Info From : MacHub

POINTS TO NOTE:

  • You will receive your commision after the customer pays for the service.

  • You can apply to earn from all the three commision options mentioned above.

  • Reusing of the commision M-codes is not permitted.

  • If you refer individuals to our website using fake M-codes without having applied for the job, you will not be eligible for any commissions.
